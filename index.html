What is mini-cv?

If you have ever felt the frustration of customizing your resume for every single job description, then mini-cv is for you. It allows you to customize your resume based on the skills you select.

With mini-cv, you write your resume once, and whenever you need to apply for a new job, you just filter out all the skills that are not related to that job description.

What you need to do:

Step 1: Write your resume, putting down everything you ever did in your professional life.

Step 2: For every line item in your resume, add skill tags related to that line. Choose these skills wisely.

Step 3: Now based on each job description you see, filter all the skills and only keep the ones that are related.

Step 4: Click on "Download" and then select "save as PDF".
